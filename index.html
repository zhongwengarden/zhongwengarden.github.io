Memorize Chinese words and phrases by planting them your own virtual garden. Over time your seeds will grow into beautiful character-flowers and word-bouquets. Once they bloom you can sell them by passing a quiz.

  • Over N characters and phrases to unlock
  • Challenges where you can unlock hidden rewards
  • Players can choose between Traditional and Simplified characters
  • Quizes on character meanings and pinyin representations
