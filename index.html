中文 Garden is a game based on memorizing Chinese words and phrases. You plant characters in a virtual garden and over time they will grow into beautiful flowers and bouquets. Once they bloom you can sell them by passing a quiz.

Download for Android

Download for iOS

Features

  • Over N characters and phrases to unlock
  • Challenges where you can unlock hidden rewards
  • Players can choose between Traditional and Simplified characters
  • Quizes on character meanings and pinyin representations
