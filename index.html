中文 Garden is a game based on memorizing Chinese words and phrases. You plant characters in a virtual garden and over time they will grow into beautiful flowers and bouquets. Once they bloom you can sell them by passing a quiz.

Download Android Version

Download iOS Version

Features

  • Earn additional prizes by completing a variety of challenges
  • Pluck weeds to study while waiting for flowers to bloom
  • Quizzes cover character meanings and transcriptions
  • Create crossword formations to earn greater profits
  • Over 200 characters and phrases to unlock
  • Players can choose between Traditional and Simplified Chinese characters
  • Pinyin and zhuyin (aka bopomofo) transcriptions are available
